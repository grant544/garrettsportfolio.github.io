Who I Am

My name is Garrett Grant, also known as Travis. I’m a 21-year-old Capricorn, born on December 22, 2002, in the picturesque Portland parish of Jamaica, at St. Ann’s Bay Hospital in St. Ann.

I’m currently in my third year at the University of Technology, Jamaica, where I’m pursuing a degree in Computer Network and Security with a focus on Cyber Security. My studies fuel my passion for understanding and safeguarding technology, and I’m excited about the opportunities in this ever-evolving field.

Outside of my academic life, I’m an avid traveler, exploring Jamaica and beyond on a student budget. I love watching movies, appreciating nature’s beauty, and engaging in creative pursuits like singing and writing essays. Despite being naturally introverted, I enjoy connecting with others and embracing new experiences.

Whether it's delving into the latest tech trends or enjoying a quiet moment in nature, I’m always eager to learn and grow.
