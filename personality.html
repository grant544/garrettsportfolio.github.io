My Strongest Trait and Impact

One of my strongest traits is that I am responsible, which significantly impacts my academic life. For instance, whenever I am in groups, I ensure that all my group members are able to get tasks done effectively and ensure that they are participating; I take charge. This trait helps me because, with the busy life of a college student, having something to help keep track of all assignments is crucial, and being responsible helps with that.

Favorite Quote

My favorite quote is: "Don't change yourself so that other people will like you. Be yourself so that the right people will love you.". This quote is from a motivational app that I use to get daily affirmations. I chose this quote because many times we try to fit into society and lose ourselves; you don't have to force yourself on people for them to like you. Just be yourself, and the right group will find you eventually.
