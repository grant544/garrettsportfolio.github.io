Site Theme

The overall theme of this portfolio is modern and sleek, with a professional but creative feel. The color palette uses vibrant gradients (purple and blue), and the design incorporates subtle hover effects and animations. The target audience includes potential employers, educators, and peers interested in web development and cyber security.

Website Features

  • Image Gallery on the 'About Me' page, showcasing travel and hobbies.
  • Interactive Resume PDF on the 'Professional' page.
  • Interactive picture on the home screen that leads to an email once clicked.
  • Social Media Link.

Web Pages & Purpose

  • Home Page: Introduction to the portfolio and navigation to other sections.
  • About Me Page: Provides a detailed overview of personal background, education, and interests.
  • Contact Page: A form or details for reaching out, including social media links.
  • Professional Page: Showcases resume, career vision, and skills for job opportunities.
  • Personality Page: Details about personal traits, strengths, and hobbies.
  • Personal Development Page: Describes personal growth, goals, and reflections.
  • Storyboard Page: Illustrates the wireframe and design process with image gallery.
  • References Page: Lists sources used and further reading.
